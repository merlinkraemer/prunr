### UAT-002: Documents folder not categorized - shows under "Other"

**Discovered:** 2026-01-13
**Phase/Plan:** 9.3-03
**Severity:** Major
**Feature:** Category detection
**Description:** Test data includes a "Documents" folder, but it appears under "Other" category instead of having its own "Documents" category.
**Expected:** Documents folder should be categorized as "Documents" (or at least not hidden in "Other")
**Actual:** Documents folder appears under "Other" category

**Note:** Current GrowthCategory enum does not include a "Documents" category. This is a feature gap rather than a bug - the categorization system was designed for cache/temp folders, not user documents.

**Root Cause:** GrowthCategory.swift defines 10 categories (Homebrew, node_modules, Library/Caches, Downloads, Docker, Spotify, Browser Cache, Mail Attachments, Trash, Other). Documents is not in this list.
